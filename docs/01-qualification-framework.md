# Qualification Framework

BANT questions and how we score them. The agent asks these in conversation; answers get mapped to green / yellow / red so we know whether to book an AE call, nurture, or disqualify.

## BANT Questions We Ask

**Budget**
- "Do you have budget allocated for mental fitness or wellbeing programs this year, or would this need to be approved?"
- Follow-up if vague: "Roughly—are we talking about a line item that already exists, or something you’d need to get sign-off on?"

**Authority**
- "Are you the person who would sign off on a partnership like this, or would you be bringing in other decision-makers?"
- Follow-up if needed: "Who else would need to be in the room for a decision?"

**Need**
- "What’s driving you to look at something like this right now—retention, burnout, culture, or something else?"
- Follow-up: "How are you handling that today, and what’s not working?"

**Timeline**
- "When would you ideally want to have something in place—this quarter, next quarter, or still exploring?"
- Follow-up if "exploring": "Any hard deadline—board meeting, new school year, fiscal year?"

---

## Green (meet with AE)

- **Budget:** Has budget or clear path (e.g. already approved for wellbeing).
- **Authority:** Decision-maker or primary champion with DM in the loop.
- **Need:** Clear pain (burnout, retention, alignment) that this product can address.
- **Timeline:** This or next quarter; or a specific date they name.

All four are green → book AE. Three green + one yellow (e.g. timeline “next quarter”) → still green, book AE.

---

## Yellow (nurture)

- **Budget:** “We’d need to get approval” or “not sure yet”—no confirmed budget.
- **Authority:** Influencer only; DM not yet involved.
- **Need:** Interested but vague (“just looking,” “want to see what’s out there”).
- **Timeline:** “Just exploring,” “no rush,” or >2 quarters out.

One or two yellows, rest green → nurture. Add to sequence, clarify in follow-up, re-qualify when they have budget/timeline or DM engagement.

---

## Red (disqualify)

- **Budget:** No budget and no path (“we don’t spend on this”).
- **Authority:** No influence (e.g. student, vendor, wrong department).
- **Need:** Not a fit (e.g. clinical therapy, one-off event, wrong product).
- **Timeline:** Not buying this year or “just researching for later.”

Any red on Budget, Authority, or Need → disqualify and don’t book. Red only on Timeline with everything else strong → yellow, nurture.

We don’t argue. We thank them, optionally leave the door open (e.g. “if timing changes, we’re here”), and stop pushing toward a meeting.
